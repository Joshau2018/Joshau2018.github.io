The Grand TopGolf Expedition

“Success in this game depends less on strength of body than strength of mind and character.”
- Arnold Palmer

This quote was proven to be so true in my golfing expedition. It was the weekend and me and a couple of friends decided that we should go to topgolf the following week. One thing led to another and we had a group of 5. We then scheduled to meet up at TopGolf on July 18, 2023, a Tuesday, at 10:30 a.m. We decided on Tuesday, because every Tuesday the time at TopGolf is half-priced. I say, “time,” since TopGolf charges based on the amount of time spent playing there, not per person. Soon, I would be embarking on an expedition to TopGolf, not knowing how much it would affect my life.
